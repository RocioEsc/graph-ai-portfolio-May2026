About me

GIS analyst bringing maps, data, and decisions closer together.

My work sits at the intersection of spatial analysis, public health, and practical digital tools. This portfolio highlights how I am extending that foundation with generative AI workflows that are transparent, useful, and responsibly designed.

About the course

An eight-week guided program for responsible AI task automation.

The course focuses on no-code AI tools for work and research productivity, with weekly live workshops, help sessions, community forums, feedback, capstone coaching, and a shareable certificate.

MODULE 01

Websites, documents & presentations

LLM foundations, Markdown, HTML, LaTeX, research websites, and AI-assisted communication assets.

MODULE 02

AI-automated data analysis

Code Interpreter, dashboards, Julius, data transformation, reports, visualizations, and spreadsheet automations.

MODULE 03

Research & text analysis

Literature reviews, classification, sentiment analysis, knowledge maps, summarization, and synthesis systems.

MODULE 04

Custom GPTs and chatbots

LLM chat architecture, context windows, RAG systems, Botpress, and research knowledge bases.

MODULE 05

AI for task automation

Responsible AI agents, email workflows, complex automation flows, and browser-based task support.

MODULE 06

Communication & outreach

Research summaries, scientific figures, diagrams, video abstracts, visualization prompts, and content scheduling.
